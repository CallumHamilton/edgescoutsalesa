What this is

High volume, high standards

Football selections across the major European leagues and international tournaments. This is a high volume service — we post regularly, but only when the research genuinely supports a bet. There's no filler.

Hybrid models

We find value through a mix of quantitative modelling and qualitative analysis, targeting markets where bookmakers believe they're strong but, in our view, have it wrong. Those edges exist across a wider range of markets than most people assume, and we're constantly developing new models and finding new angles to stay ahead.

Big bets

We post big bets when we find good opportunities to compound value at higher odds — not on a fixed schedule, but when the edge is there. From just ten advised so far this season, the big bets are massively in profit — headlined by a 54/1 winner.

Keeping you in the loop

Bets are sent directly to subscribers via Telegram — fast, simple, and no inbox clutter. You also receive a weekly email with our thoughts, updates on how things are going, and some of the thinking behind our approach. You'll always have a clear picture of how the service is performing and where we're focusing.

Transparent records

Everything is logged from day one to flat stakes and is independently proofed. No inflating a winning month by suddenly upping the stake, no shrinking it on losers. The record you see is the complete, unmanipulated record.

Getting bets on

A large number of our selections are available in betting shops across multiple UK bookmakers — we're not reliant on any one book, and our bets are spread across different firms. Selections are usually available at more than one bookie, and we sometimes offer alternative bets from the same angle to give you options if you're restricted or prefer a different market. A small number of selections also have exchange angles for those who need them.
